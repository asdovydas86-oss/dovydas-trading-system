## AI Financial Intelligence Platform -- Vision Update

Date: 2026-07-16

### Mission

The goal is not to build only a trading bot.

The goal is to build a personal AI Financial Intelligence Platform that
becomes my daily operating system for investing, market research, AI
learning, portfolio management and eventually carefully controlled
automation.

## Core Principles

-   AI explains WHY, not only WHAT.
-   News, macro and market structure are analyzed together.
-   Technical analysis remains a core pillar.
-   Every decision is based on probabilities, not certainty.
-   Capital preservation comes first.
